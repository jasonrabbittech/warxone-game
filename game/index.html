How to Play

WARXONE: CONQUER OR FALL

Prologue – The Day the Concord Died

For ten years, the Concord kept the peace. Every country was ranked. Every resource tracked. Every border respected.

Then came The Cracking. A single line of corrupted code spread through the Concord's core. Borders became suggestions. Armies became digital tokens.

The Concord was never built for peace. It was built to find the person ruthless enough to break it.

You are that person.

"Welcome to WarXone. Declare war. Conquer countries. No limits."

⚠️ MARS DISCOVERED ⚠️

Part 7 – The Red Signal

With Earth nearly under your control, a deep-space transmission is intercepted. It's a challenge from the Martian Colonial Authority — 50 sovereign nations, united under one banner, daring you to conquer the Red Planet.

"You've taken Earth. But can you survive Mars?"

The Martian nations have formed the Red Concord — an alliance specifically designed to counter your tactics. Each nation is fortified with advanced technology, harsh terrain, and a burning hatred for Earth's conqueror.

Mars is colder. Deadlier. And waiting.

New objective: Conquer all 50 Martian nations.
